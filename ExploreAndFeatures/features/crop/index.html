Rice Cultivation Information

Requirements for Rice Cultivation

Rice is a staple food for a large part of the world's population. Here are the key requirements for successful rice cultivation:

  • Climate: Warm, tropical climates with temperatures between 20°C and 40°C are ideal for rice growth.
  • Water: Rice requires a significant amount of water, often grown in flooded fields known as paddies.
  • Soil: Clay or silty clay soils with good water retention are best for rice cultivation.
  • Sunlight: Abundant sunlight is necessary for photosynthesis and healthy growth.
  • Growing Season: A long growing season of 3 to 6 months is required for rice to mature.

Market Value

The global rice market is significant, with rice being one of the most widely consumed staple foods. Here are some market insights:

  • Global Production: Over 513 million metric tons of milled rice were produced worldwide in the last harvesting year.
  • Leading Producers: China, India, and Bangladesh are the top producers of rice.
  • Market Size: The global rice market is estimated at USD 376.54 billion in 2024 and is expected to reach USD 436.51 billion by 2029.
